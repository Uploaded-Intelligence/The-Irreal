# Â§8 DESIGN PRINCIPLES

```
1. DISCOVERY OVER DIRECTION
   Don't tell people where to go. Make them curious about where they could go.

2. REWARDS FOR EXPLORATION  
   Hidden things. Secrets. Easter eggs. The player who explores finds more.

3. THE SPACE COMMUNICATES
   Environmental storytelling. The world itself tells you things.
   Before you read any text, the atmosphere has already spoken.

4. COHERENT BUT VARIED
   Unified world, but distinct regions. You always know you're in The Irreal,
   but each zone has its own character.

5. ALIVE, NOT STATIC
   Things move. Respond. Change. Ambient life. Not a museumâ€”a living world.

6. GENEROUS DEPTH
   Surface is accessible. Depth rewards engagement. 
   First visit: get the vibe. Return visits: discover layers.

7. WORKING IN PUBLIC
   Seedlings are visible. Growth is visible. The world is explicitly becoming.
   Not polished facadeâ€”honest process.

8. PLURALITY IS ARCHITECTURAL
   The multiple selves aren't explainedâ€”they're EXPERIENCED as different spaces.
   You navigate plurality, not read about it.
```
